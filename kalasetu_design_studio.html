Our Story

Designing silence
into every room.

Kala Setu Design Studio was founded on a single belief — that the most powerful interior is one you don't immediately notice. It simply makes you feel at ease. Every decision, from the soffit height to the thread count of fabric, is made in service of that feeling.

Based across India's finest residential corridors, we work on projects from Delhi to Mumbai, Jodhpur to Bangalore — bringing the same restraint and rigour to every brief, regardless of scale.

We are architects of atmosphere. Our practice spans civil construction, interior architecture, joinery, lighting design, and landscape — all coordinated through a single creative vision.

Why choose turnkey

Why should you give
us complete control?

Because managing a construction site while living your life is one of the most exhausting things a person can do. Coordinating 12 different contractors who don't talk to each other — it always leads to delays, cost overruns, and regret.

When you hand us the keys, you get one phone number, one timeline, one budget — and accountability for all of it.

Start a Turnkey Project
  • Single point of accountabilityOne team, one contract. No finger-pointing between trades.
  • Fixed-price contractsWe lock the budget upfront. No surprise invoices mid-construction.
  • Vendor relationships12 years of trusted craftsmen, stone suppliers, and furniture makers pass savings on to you.
  • Guaranteed timelinePenalty clauses built into every turnkey contract. We are incentivized to finish on time.
  • Zero coordination burden on youYou attend two design reviews. We handle the rest — including site visits and punch lists.
  • Cohesive outcomeWhen one team designs and builds everything, the result is seamless. Design integrity never gets lost in translation.
